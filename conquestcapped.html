Services Payment Safety
What is ConquestCapped?
We started as a team of professional WoW players with the experience dating back to the late Burning Crusade. We started providing boosting services in 2015 with selling arena rating boosts, then we quickly expanded to cover all sorts of services including raiding, dungeons and powerleveling. Currently our team has several hundreds players of different specializations capable of fulfilling any order.
What is your work schedule?
Players on our large team have very flexible schedule and are available 24/7/365. Customer support & Sales team works every day 24/7. We are fully operational even during holidays.
How does your service work?
After you have chosen the service you are directed to the checkout page where you leave your contact details and select payment method, then you pay for the service. At this point we start working on your order, our Support Team will contact you within 15 minutes to clarify all details.
What is Account Sharing and Selfplay?
These are two different boosting methods. These are Account sharing, also known as Piloting, means that our player will be logging in your game account to fulfill the service. This boosting method is used for services that cannot be completed as group such as solo questing, etc. We will never ask for any sensitive account data such as answer to secret question, we only need login and password. Selfplay means that you will be playing on your own, our players will meet you in game to provide their help.
Do you offer custom services?
We do! If you are looking for something that isn't available on our website feel free to ask our Support Team and we'll prepare an offer. We have the experience of completing large and complex orders such as PvP Rank 14 in WoW Classic, gathering complete Tier 3 set from Black Market in WoW and many more.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa/Mastercard and other payment methods such as Apple/GooglePay, Sofort, etc.. For large custom orders alternative payment methods such as bank transfer are negotiable.
Do you accept gold or other in-game currency?
No, we don't.
Do you offer payment plans?
In general we accept only full upfront payments, but splitting the payment in several smaller transactions might be negotiable for large custom orders.
What happens after purhcase?
After you have placed the order, our Support Team will contact you within 15 minutes.
Is it possible to get a refund?
If you ordered a wrong service or want to cancel an order before it was started, just ask our Support Team and we'll try to offer a compromise solution or just completely cancel your order and issue a refund. For complete information on our refund policy check the Terms of Service page.
Won't you just disappear with the money?
First of all, we've been in this business since 2015, registered a company and collected huge number of customer reviews, there's no point for us in doing petty scams as we're looking to establish long-term relationships with our customers. Secondly, we process all payments through secure payment systems, all of which have a dispute mechanism. It is technically not possible for a seller to disappear with the money, buyer can open a dispute and is guaranteed a refund unless the seller proves the service actually was delivered.
Who are your players?
Over the years, we have assembled a team of trusted and competent players who can complete any task. Each of them has passed our internal review process before starting to actually receive orders. Our management team strives to makes customer experience as smooth as possible and ensures that only the best players are assigned to orders.
Is it safe to share my account details with you?
If the service requires account sharing, will never ask for any sensitive account data such as answer to secret question, etc. which makes it technically impossible to hijack the account, we will only request the minimun information required to log on your account, that being username and password. And of course we will NEVER use any shady boosting methods such as botting, exploiting, and other forms of cheating while playing on your account!
I don't want to share my account.
For most services we provide a selfplay option either free of charge, or at some extra cost. However, certain services can be completed only with account sharing due to their nature (solo questing, farming, etc.).
